Characters can now link more than one Daz scene. Adding a scene from outside the character folder offers to copy or move it into a chosen subfolder, the scene folder can be relinked if it is renamed outside the app, and each scene can be unlinked (optionally deleting it from disk). Every scene shows as a card with its Daz `.tip.png` portrait, and clicking it opens the scene in Daz Studio.
