Remember mapped network drives (X: → \\host\share) as you pick paths and re-map any that are missing on startup — so the app keeps working after you relaunch it as administrator, when Windows hides your interactive drive mappings from the elevated session. A new "Network drives" section in Settings → General lists them with their status, a manual re-map, and a Forget action.
