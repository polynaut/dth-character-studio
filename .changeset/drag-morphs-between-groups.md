Drag morphs between groups. The pose drag-and-drop now spans a whole section instead of being locked to one group, so you can move a morph (pose) from one group into another — drop it on a pose to insert at that spot, or on an empty group's body to append — not just reorder within a group. A drag overlay shows the morph you're moving. Handy after a CSV import to redistribute morphs across groups.
