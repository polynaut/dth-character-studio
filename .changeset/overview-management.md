Overhauled the project and character overviews with management controls. Both now have a **grid / list** view toggle and **sort** (name, newest, oldest); the character overview adds **Genesis** and **Gender** filters. Items are **selectable** — the per-item trash button is gone; instead, selecting one or more reveals a bulk-action bar with **Delete**, which opens a confirm modal (for characters, with options to **keep the Daz / Houdini files** on disk). Each character now also has a **Special operations** pane with **Clone** (duplicate into a new copy) and **Delete**.
